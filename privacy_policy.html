General Information

Galaxy Vanguard is an offline game developed by thpereiras that prioritizes user privacy. This document explains how we handle data in our application.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect personal or sensitive data directly. However, the game includes advertising services and in-app purchases that may collect certain information as described below.

Local Storage

The application uses local device storage only for:

  • Game settings: Audio preferences, language, and controls
  • Player progress: Unlocked levels and high scores
  • Game statistics: Achievements and match statistics

All this data stays exclusively on your device and is not shared.

System Features

Screen Control

  • The game uses "keep screen active" functionality (wakelock) during matches to prevent the screen from turning off automatically
  • This functionality is only activated during gameplay and serves exclusively to improve user experience

Audio and Multimedia

  • The application plays sound effects and background music
  • Does not access microphone or record user audio

Permissions

The application does not request special Android permissions beyond those necessary for:

  • Audio playback
  • Local preference storage
  • Screen power control (keep active during games)

Advertising and Third-Party Services

Advertisement Integration

  • Interstitial Ads: The game displays ads between levels
  • Ad-Free Option: Users can purchase an ad-removal feature through in-app purchases
  • Ad Networks: We use third-party advertising services (such as AdMob) that may collect device information for ad personalization

In-App Purchases

  • Remove Ads: One-time purchase to disable all advertisements
  • Purchase Processing: Handled by Google Play Store - we do not store payment information
  • Purchase Verification: We verify purchases through Google Play's secure systems

Data Collected by Third Parties

Advertising partners may collect:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • App usage patterns for ad targeting
  • IP address for geographic targeting

Note: This data is collected by third-party services, not by us directly. Users can opt out of personalized advertising through their device settings.

Services We Do NOT Use

  • We do not integrate with social networks
  • There is no login or user accounts
  • We do not use analytics beyond ad services

Privacy Controls

Opt-Out Options

  • Personalized Ads: Disable through Android Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • All Ads: Purchase the "Remove Ads" option in the game
  • Reset Advertising ID: Available in Android Settings > Google > Ads

Children's Privacy

Our game is designed to be family-friendly. For users under 13:

  • Ads are filtered to be age-appropriate
  • No personal data is collected directly by our app
  • Parents can disable ads entirely through the in-app purchase

Security

Locally saved data is protected by standard Android system security measures. Third-party advertising services use industry-standard security practices. We do not store or transmit personal data ourselves.

Minors

The game is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, there are no specific privacy concerns related to minors.

Changes to This Policy

We will notify about significant changes to this policy through app updates or announcements in the project repository.

Data Deletion

To completely remove all game data:

  1. Uninstall the application
  2. Locally saved data will be automatically removed
